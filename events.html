Arts and Crafts

Spero volunteers teach fun, hands-on arts and crafts projects to seniors. Youtube channel coming soon.




Postcards

Spero volunteers write festive messages on cards that go out to families in need! Postcards are also sent to hospitals that have many young patients.


IN2L

Spero volunteers play crossword on an interactive whiteboard with dementia patients. Postponed due to the pandemic.



Pen Pals

Spero volunteers exchange letters about their life's experiences and wisdom with seniors. Volunteers also make postcards with friendly greetings that are sent to seniors.
