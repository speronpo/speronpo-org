Our Goal

Helping the Community

SPERO is an incorporated nonprofit organization that focuses on bringing seniors closer to the rest of the community, allowing them to contribute and connect with society. Currently, SPERO hosts events at two different senior centers, and we are looking to expand to more locations as well as add more members to the organization.
